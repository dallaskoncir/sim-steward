# Official Sim Racing League Sporting Code

## 1.0 Overtaking and Defending
- **1.1 The Vortex of Danger:** If an attacking car attempts a pass on the inside of a corner but does not have their front axle alongside the defending car's B-pillar before the turn-in point, the attacking car is entirely at fault for any resulting contact. Penalty: 10s to Drive-Through.
- **1.2 Moving Under Braking:** Defending drivers may make one defensive move on a straight. However, altering your line once you have entered the braking zone to block an attacking car is strictly prohibited. Penalty: 5s to 10s.
- **1.3 Squeezing:** Forcing a car off the track limits on the exit of a corner when they have established significant overlap is not permitted. You must leave exactly one car width of space. Penalty: 5s.

## 2.0 Track Limits and Rejoining
- **2.1 Unsafe Rejoin:** Rejoining the racing surface after an off-track excursion in a manner that forces approaching cars to lift off the throttle or take evasive action is a severe safety hazard. Penalty: 20s to Stop-and-Go.
- **2.2 Corner Cutting:** Gaining a lasting time advantage by exceeding track limits (all four tires over the white line) to execute a pass requires the driver to give the position back within one lap. Failure to do so: 10s Penalty.

## 3.0 Pit Lane and Grid Procedures
- **3.1 Pit Speeding:** Exceeding the pit lane speed limit during a race session by less than 10kph will result in a 15-second post-race time penalty. Exceeding it by more than 10kph results in an automatic Drive-Through penalty.
- **3.2 Pit Exit Blending:** Crossing the solid white line on the pit exit while blending into active race traffic is prohibited, regardless of whether a collision occurs. Penalty: 5s.

## 4.0 Conduct and Connectivity
- **4.1 Brake Testing:** Intentionally applying brakes unpredictably on a straight to force a trailing car to react or collide is unsportsmanlike conduct. Penalty: Immediate Disqualification (DSQ).
- **4.2 Retaliation:** Intentional contact out of frustration, whether during the race or post-race before the server transitions, is strictly forbidden. Penalty: DSQ and 2-race ban.
- **4.3 Netcode Incidents:** If replay telemetry and server logs confirm that a collision was caused purely by server latency or packet loss (netcode), and a visible gap existed between the car models, no penalty will be assigned.
