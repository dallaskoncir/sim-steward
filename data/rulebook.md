# Sim Racing Penalty Guidelines

## Track Limits Violation
Exceeding track limits (all four wheels beyond the white line) more than three times in a race results in a 5-second time penalty. A gained advantage from a track limits violation must be relinquished immediately or a drive-through penalty will be applied.

## Causing a Collision
A driver deemed fully responsible for causing a collision with another car will receive a 10-second stop-go penalty. If the collision ends another driver's race, the penalty escalates to a post-race 30-second time penalty and two penalty points on their license.

## Unsafe Rejoin
Rejoining the track in a manner that endangers other drivers, including rejoining without checking mirrors or crossing the racing line while significantly slower than surrounding traffic, results in a 5-second time penalty plus one penalty point.

## Ignoring Blue Flags
Failing to yield to a lapping car within three corners of a blue flag being shown results in a 5-second time penalty per infraction. Repeated offenses in the same race escalate to a drive-through penalty.
